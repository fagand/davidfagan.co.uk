About Me

I am years old. I have always had a passion for computers and technology. When I was around 15, I created my first website. I used my graphic design skills to design a layout, I then sliced and coded the layout, secured hosting and finally uploaded the site to the world wide web. The website went on to have a period of great popularity which only increased my desire to make it last. The site today pays homage to the past. I hope in the future to come up with a plan to provide the music content again. In the past, the site progressed and improved with aim to keep it modern and appealing.
My passion for technology has most recently led me into further education where I graduated as BSc of Computing from Glasgow Caledonian University.
Prior to this, I worked for Headlam, the biggest flooring distributor in Europe. I worked and progress there in various roles. I was at Headlam for more than 7 years, during this time, I developed as a person and an employee. I matured gaining valuable work skills that I can transfer into my future career.

I have experience in two object orientated programming languages, Java and C#. As well as this I have built on my previous experience of HTML, adding JavaScript and jQuery experience.
I have many years of work experience with customer relations and team working.

My work history and skills can be viewed on my CV, please contact me using one of the contact options to request my CV.
